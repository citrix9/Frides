Frequently Asked Questions 

 Welcome to Frides!

What is Frides?

Autopooling helps you find people who live around you and are also going 
to your college, work and events, in order to share a car with them (carpool). 
This allows you to save nature,money and fuel while meeting new friends, and contributing 
to reduce traffic and air pollution.

Is Frides Free?

Using the website is free, however we leave it up the participants to split the price of fuel fairly 
among themselves.

Is Frides Safe?

Before sharing a trip with a stranger, you are advised to discuss by private messages and check out 
that person's Facebook profile if he/she has shared it.

Who Can Register On Frides?

Anyone can register via their Google's mail and start using our services.
